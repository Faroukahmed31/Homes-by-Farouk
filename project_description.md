# Project Description: Homes by Farouk Website

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** Homes by Farouk
**Business Type:** Real Estate Agency (Rentals, Sales, and Off-Plan Investments)
**Location Focus:** Nairobi, Kenya (e.g., Westlands, Kilimani, Kileleshwa, Lavington, Syokimau, South B, South C)
**Primary Objective:** To build a highly effective, modern real estate website that serves as a conversion engine. The site will showcase property listings, build trust through authoritative content, and seamlessly capture leads for local buyers, renters, and foreign/diaspora investors.

## 2. Brand Identity & Design
* **Primary Palette:** Deep Black (often as a marble texture) and Rich Gold. This conveys luxury, authority, and professionalism.
* **Action Color:** Cardinal Red. Used *exclusively* for high-priority Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons (e.g., "Book a Viewing", "Download Guide") to create high contrast and urgency.
* **Vibe:** Modern, energetic, and structurally sound, matching the geometric, architectural logo design.

## 3. Website Architecture (Sitemap)
The website will feature a streamlined, low-friction structure consisting of the following core pages:

* **Landing Page (Homepage):**
  * **Hero Section:** High-quality background property imagery, strong headline, and a Cardinal Red CTA.
  * **Quick Search/Filter:** Front-and-center toggle for Rent vs. Buy and Off-Plan vs. Ready-to-Move-In.
  * **Featured Listings:** A carousel of 3–6 top properties (mix of everyday and high-end).
  * **Lead Magnet Split:** Two clear pathways to capture emails (Local Buyer Guide & Foreign Investor Guide).
* **Properties Page:** * Full catalog of listings with advanced filtering (price, bedrooms, location).
  * Visual status badges on property cards (e.g., Green for "Ready", Gold for "Off-Plan").
* **About Us:** * Focuses on Farouk’s passion, modern market awareness, and dedication to helping clients make smart financial decisions (avoiding the mention of being a 1-year agent, instead highlighting fresh expertise and investor-first mindset).
* **Contact Us:** * Direct contact details (Phone, Email, WhatsApp) and a general inquiry form.

## 4. Key Features & Functionality
### Frictionless Communication
* **Sticky WhatsApp Button:** A floating "Chat with us" WhatsApp icon visible on every page of the website.
* **Smart WhatsApp Routing:** Clicking the WhatsApp button on a property listing automatically opens a pre-filled message specifying the property (e.g., *"Hi Farouk, I am interested in the 2-bedroom apartment in Kilimani..."*).
* **Contact Quick Links:** Every listing ends with a highly visible "Homes by Farouk" contact block featuring the phone number (+254721599075), email (faroukahmed3121@gmail.com), and quick-tap Call/WhatsApp buttons.

### Listing Page UX
* **Dynamic Contact Forms:** The message placeholder text changes based on the property type:
  * *Ready Units:* "I'm interested in [Property Name] 4 bedroom."
  * *Off-Plan Units:* "I'm interested in knowing about the payment plans available for [Property Name]."
* **Gated Off-Plan Pricing:** Off-plan listings display a "Starting From" price but require users to click "Get Payment Plan Details" (entering Name, Email, Phone) to automatically receive the installment breakdown.

## 5. Lead Generation Strategy (Lead Magnets)
To capture emails and build authority, the site will heavily utilize automated lead magnets. When a user submits their details (Name, Email, Phone), the respective PDF guide is automatically emailed to them.

1. **The Nairobi Real Estate Playbook: 5 Costly Mistakes Buyers Make**
   * *Target:* Local buyers and investors.
   * *Content:* Tips on buying off-plan, the importance of location, the value of multiple smaller units vs. one large unit, upcoming infrastructure growth, and Airbnb potential.
2. **The Complete Guide to Investing in Nairobi Real Estate as a Foreigner**
   * *Target:* Diaspora and expat investors.
   * *Content:* Explanation of the 99-year leasehold, KRA PIN requirements, the necessity of a local advocate, and the highest ROI neighborhoods (Westlands, Kilimani, Syokimau, etc.).
